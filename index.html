Welcome to Page of Demo CRM.

Demo CRM is designed for a small business tracking of clients, agreements and payments. It has two main parts

Currently code at heavy refactoring, so take a look at screenshots!

1. Clients part

Client can

  • View his agreements in short or extanded (full details)
  • View status of agreements - payed or not
  • View his payments in short or extanded (full details)
  • View his info

2. User (employee) part

User can

  • View, add, remove his clients
  • View, add, remove agreements of his clients
  • Attach/detach existing payments to agreements
  • View banks accounts info
  • etc

User interface is designed with help of Wordpress Bootstrap Framework and heavy usage of JavaScript and jQuery.
